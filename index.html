FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions Homeowners Often Ask

We understand selling a home is a big decision. Here are answers to some of the most common questions we receive from homeowners.

Do I need to make repairs before selling?

No. We purchase properties as-is, so there is no need to spend time or money on repairs before selling.

Are there any commissions or hidden fees?

No. We do not charge realtor commissions and there are no hidden fees.

How quickly can you close?

Every situation is different, but we strive to close as quickly as possible while working around your preferred timeline.

Is there any obligation after requesting an offer?

No. Requesting an offer is completely free and there is never any obligation to accept.

Which areas do you buy in?

We work with homeowners throughout Virginia and surrounding communities.

What types of properties do you buy?

We consider a variety of residential properties including single-family homes, townhomes, inherited properties, rental properties, and homes needing repairs.

Can I sell a property with tenants still living there?

In many cases, yes. Contact us to discuss your specific situation and we'll review available options.

How do you determine your offer?

Our offer is based on several factors including property condition, location, market conditions, and any repairs that may be needed.

What if my property needs major repairs?

That's okay. We frequently work with properties that need repairs, updates, or extensive renovations.

Can I choose my closing date?

Yes. We aim to make the process flexible and work around a closing schedule that fits your needs whenever possible.
